The Debate Club

Managing Partner and champion of multiple debate competitions

Debate Competitions

In my Sophomore year I began competing in local debate competitions to further better my speaking abilities. In my first regional competition my team narrowly won the finals to win first place. I went on to compete in other local competitions; winning some, losing others. Soon I took on the moderating role, and became the Chief Moderator at the Eastern Province Inter-school Debate competition.

Managing Partner

With my extensive debating skills, I quickly rose through the ranks to become one of two Managing Partners of the Debate Club by Junior year. As a senior executive, I coached young members on how to speak effectively and use advanced debate rhetoric. I lead weekly meetings in which members would practice and I would help them in becoming award winning debaters. It was also my responsibility to organize our school's annual Eastern Province Interschool Debate competition. I resigned my role as Managing Partner in Senior year due to my commitments to MUN and MIT LaunchX.

How I Started

My first encounter with debating was when I began doing Model United Nations (MUN), which had a lot of debating aspects included within it. Debating and arguing was always enjoyable to me, and so when I was in High School I joined the Debate Club.

Speakathon

The Debate Club holds annual speakathon competitions, in which the best speakers from the school would give insirational speeches in front of the entire student body. I competed in my first one in Sophomore year as the youngest competitior. Though I lost I took a lot from the event, and when I competed in Junior year, I won second place. I hope to compete again this year and take the first place.

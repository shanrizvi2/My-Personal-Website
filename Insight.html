Insight

Co-Founder and Business Adminstrator of Insight: the world's first Virtual Reality tutoring program

The Project

Insight is a Virtual Reality program that connects students with a tutor of their choice and takes them into a personalized classroom with resources that aren't accessible in the real world. With the introduction of VR labs, Insight allows students to interact with their learning in a fun and exciting way.

Where We Are Going From Here

We truly believe that Insight can change education, making it more fun and interactive using modern technology, and that is why we are in contact with other educational companies and start-ups to make our project public. If you are interested in working with us, or licensing our project, please contact me (shanrizvi4@hotmail.com) or Omar Shaikh (oshaikh13@gmail.com).

My Role

There are two parts of a start-up, the programming aspect, and the business and creative aspect. While my partner was the programmer, I provided innovative ideas to take Insight to the next level. As the business adminstator I developed a detailed business plan that outlined every revenue source, every cost, and every method we would use to create user interest: I outlined how the start-up would run.

Microsoft's Imagine Cup

We submitted our project in Microsoft's Imagine Cup, an innovation competition for student developers, and beat 400 university students to take the Saudi National Trophy. We then traveled to Beirut, Lebanon for the Middle Eastern Finals, where we lost but were inspired by the brilliance of our competitors.
